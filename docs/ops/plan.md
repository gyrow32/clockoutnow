# Comms AI Services — Business Plan

## The Opportunity
Buffalo Craigslist computer services: ~16 competitors, ALL doing traditional web design/SEO. **Zero** are selling AI chatbots, automation, or agents. We own this lane.

## Target Customer
Local service businesses posting on Buffalo CL who need tech but don't know it yet:
- **Tier 1 (Hot):** Emergency/24-7 services — HVAC, plumbing, water cleanup, towing. *These businesses lose money every missed call. AI chatbot = instant ROI.*
- **Tier 2 (Warm):** Appointment-based — cleaning, landscaping, roofing, salons. *Booking automation saves them hours/week.*
- **Tier 3 (Cool):** General small biz — shops, restaurants, machine shops. *Website + online presence play.*

## What We Sell (Priority Order)

### 1. AI Chatbot — "Never Miss a Lead" ($500 setup + $99/mo)
- 24/7 AI assistant on their website that answers customer questions, captures leads, books appointments
- Trained on their specific business info (hours, services, pricing, FAQs)
- Text/email notifications for every new lead
- **Pitch:** "Your competitors have someone answering at 2am. Do you?"

### 2. Website + AI Combo ($1,200 setup + $99/mo)
- Modern responsive website + chatbot bundled
- Perfect for businesses with NO web presence (most CL posters)
- Hosting, SSL, domain included in monthly
- **Pitch:** "Right now your online presence is a Craigslist ad. Let's fix that."

### 3. Workflow Automation ($800-2,000 one-time)
- Automate scheduling, invoicing, follow-ups, review requests
- n8n or custom scripts connected to their existing tools
- **Pitch:** "You're spending 10 hours/week on stuff a robot could do in 10 seconds."

### 4. Custom Dev (Quote-based, $2,000+)
- For bigger fish — dashboards, APIs, Shopify, scraping
- Only pursue when they come to us or after trust is built

## Two-Pronged Strategy

### Inbound: CL Ads
- **Ad 1 — AI Chatbot angle:** "Your Business Loses Leads at 2am. Ours Doesn't." Target service businesses.
- **Ad 2 — Website angle:** "Need a Website That Actually Gets You Customers?" Target businesses with no web presence.
- **Ad 3 — Automation angle:** "Still Doing [Task] Manually? There's a Better Way." Target businesses drowning in admin.
- Post in: services > computer, sm biz ads
- Repost every 2 weeks per market
- Link to website with contact form

### Outbound: Direct Outreach
- Daily: Scan CL sm biz ads for service companies
- Research each: Do they have a website? Google reviews? Social media?
- Score leads: No website = hot. Bad website = warm. Good website = skip.
- Reply with tailored pitch showing how we solve THEIR specific problem
- **Chrissy (AI) handles:** Lead scanning, research, pitch drafting, CL monitoring
- **Mike/Abuzar handle:** Final outreach, sales calls, delivery
