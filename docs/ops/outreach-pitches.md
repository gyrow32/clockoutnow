## Notes on Outreach
- **Keep it short** — CL replies that are walls of text get ignored
- **Ask a question first** — Gets them thinking about the problem before you offer the solution
- **Local angle always** — "I'm right here in WNY" builds trust
- **No jargon** — "AI assistant" not "RAG-powered LLM agent"
- **Free demo offer** — Low commitment, high conversion
- **Sign as Mike** — Personal, not corporate
